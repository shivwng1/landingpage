1.1 Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.

We offer refunds for unused subscription time on a pro-rated basis. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.
